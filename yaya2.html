My top favourite genre: Kpop!

I'm a huge fan of K-Pop! Here are my favorite groups:

NCT 127

NCT 127 is the first fixed sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed by SM Entertainment. The group debuted on July 7, 2016. They consist of 8 members. They are one of the NCT unit. And although I adore and love all the members and the units, NCT 127 hold a special place in my heart the most.

NCT Dream

NCT Dream is the third sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed by SM Entertainment. The group debuted on August 25, 2016. They are known for their youthful concepts and energetic performances. This unit is my safe space too. They have 7 members in total.

Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name. The group debuted on March 25, 2018. I started stanning them on 9th October 2019 after watching Double Knot MV on YouTube. My bias in Stray Kids is Hwang Hyunjin. They originally have 9 members, but since one member left the group due to personal reason, Stray Kids now has 8 members only and s famous with motto eight is fate.

Seventeen

Seventeen is a South Korean boy band formed by Pledis Entertainment. The group debuted on May 26, 2015. They are known for their synchronized performances and are self-producing idols, involved in writing and producing their own music. Seventeen has 13 members and is divided to 3 units which are Hip-Hop Unit, Performance Unit and Vocal Unit.

Cortis

Cortis is an up-and-coming K-Pop group known for their unique musical style and captivating performances. They have been gaining popularity for their innovative concepts and powerful stage presence. This group is very unique because most of their songs are self-produced and they event shot the MVs by themselves.
